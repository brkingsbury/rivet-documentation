Overview

The Rivet Design System is a collection of standardized components and visual styles (icons, colors, typography). Built in collaboration with our development and design teams, Advicent can now quickly create beautiful, simple, and consistent user experiences across all our products.

Rivet mission statement

Rivet's mission is to provide helpful, high quality, and cohesive resources to ensure delightful user experiences

Rivet values

Deliver high quality components and clearly defined styles:

Creating great experiences starts with reusable components and styles. Through iteration, testing, and review, Rivet will maintain a high standard for usability across our products.

Maintain transparent and conistent documentation:

Documentation will clearly communicate the standards of the design system. As the source of truth, Rivet will be accessible to our product teams.
