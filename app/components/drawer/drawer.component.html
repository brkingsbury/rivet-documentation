Drawer

Drawers are containers that slide in from the side.

Use Case

Use Drawers to contain important information or settings that are not needed to be shown directly on the page. Drawers are triggered open using a button or tile and closed by clicking ‘Close’ within.

Implementation

Drawers, by default, do not have a button added to close the drawer (in most cases an 'x'). To do this add a button with a click listener that calls toggleDrawer().

In some cases it may be desirable to have the apron close on an apron click event (or additionally fire a confirmation of some sort). To allow this, simply hook a function into the apronClickEvent output.
